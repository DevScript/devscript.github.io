    Ambassador Program

    Being an FSS Ambassador, your goal will be to put in your creative thoughts to promote the conference, and spread the word by sharing the resources so that others can be benefitted.

    Plus, this is how the network is built. There's no age barrier, or skill set required, anyone with a will to learn and contribute to the community is welcome. Get your friends and peers to join us in this biggest Flagship event of DevScript.
